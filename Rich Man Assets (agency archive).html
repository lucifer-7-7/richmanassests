(06) — How we work

Four steps.
No surprises.

A deliberately small process — built by a team that has been on the legal, banking and developer side of the table, often at the same time.

i.
Step 01 of 04
i.
Listen, before we show anything.

A 30-minute conversation. Use case, budget, timeline, family situation. We do not put inventory in front of you until we understand the brief.

30 min
ii.
Shortlist a curated three to five.

We pick from our books — never the firehose. Each option personally walked, photographed and documented before it lands on your desk.

3–7 days
iii.
Diligence, with legal & finance in parallel.

Title, RERA, RTC, encumbrance — verified by Adv. Deepa R. Kotian. Bank sanction file moves in parallel through Ajay Pandit's finance desk.

2–3 weeks
iv.
Close, and stay in the file.

Registration, possession, fit-out. We stay until the keys are in your hand — and after, for the inevitable second purchase.
