Above the mist

Wake to a valley filling with cloud.

Kopparige Hills stands at the top of its own ridge, and the infinity pool simply hands the river valley over to you. On still mornings the mist sits below the deck and burns off slowly while you have your coffee.

The house is built for the view — long glazed rooms, deep verandahs, and an edge-pool that disappears into the horizon line. Nights are cool enough for a blanket, even in summer.

This is hill country: coffee, cardamom and birdsong, with the nearest town a comfortable drive down the ghat.
