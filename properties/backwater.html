Stepping to the water

Terraces that walk down to the river.

The Backwater House reads best from the water — a series of planted terraces and glazed rooms stepping down the bank to the river, with a hanging garden trailing over the edge.

Inside, split levels follow the slope, so nearly every room has its own outlook over the water and the palms on the far bank. A deck sits right at the river's edge.

Quiet, private, and properly connected to its setting — a house that lives with the tide.
