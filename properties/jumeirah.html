Made for gathering

A brick pavilion opening onto an enormous lawn.

The Lawn House is built for people — a long brick pavilion with a clay-tiled roof that opens completely onto one of the largest private lawns on this coast. A glazed colonnade runs the length of the garden.

It's equally a family home and a place for celebrations: weddings, reunions, the kind of gathering that needs room and a roof that can stay open to the evening.

Padubidri's Blue-Flag beach is a short drive, and there's parking and back-of-house to match the scale. Available on long lease.
