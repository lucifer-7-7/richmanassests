(06) — How We Work

Four steps.
No surprises.

A deliberately small process — built by a team that has been on the legal, banking and developer side of the table, often at the same time.

i.
Listen

A 30-minute conversation. Use case, budget, timeline, family situation. We do not show anything yet.

ii.
Shortlist

We curate three to five live options from our books — never the firehose. Each one personally walked.

iii.
Diligence

Title, RERA, RTC, encumbrance — checked by Advocate Deepa R. Kotian. Bank pre-approval in parallel.

iv.
Close

Registration, possession, fit-out. We stay in the file until the keys are in your hand — and after.
